Letter Hunt

p5.js · Interactive · Street Photography

Hunt for letters hidden in street photography from Bengaluru. Each letter leads you closer to a fruity reward.

How to Play

Look for letters in each image. Hover to highlight, click to progress.

The letters you find will determine your final destination.

Press R to restart the game.
