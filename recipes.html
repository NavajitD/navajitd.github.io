Perfect Pasta Aglio e Olio

Serves 2 · 15 minutes

A classic Italian pasta dish with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. Simple, quick, and incredibly flavorful. This is my go-to when I want something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen.

Key Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Red chili flakes to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Sunday Morning Pancakes

Serves 4 · 20 minutes

Fluffy buttermilk pancakes that make lazy Sunday mornings special. The secret is not overmixing the batter and letting it rest for a few minutes before cooking.

Key Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter

One-Pot Chicken Biryani

Serves 4 · 45 minutes

A simplified version of the classic Indian rice dish, cooked entirely in one pot. Aromatic basmati rice layered with spiced chicken and caramelized onions.

Key Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Biryani spice mix (garam masala, cinnamon, cardamom)
  • Saffron and milk for color
  • Fresh coriander and mint
